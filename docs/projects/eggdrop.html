Required Calculations

For your Google Form reflection, you must calculate the following values for BOTH drops. Assume g = 9.8 m/sÂ².

1. Velocity & Energy

Velocity without Device (Freefall):

v = √(2gh)

Actual Velocity at Impact:

(Calculate using your drop time t)

vf = g × t

Energy at the Top:

PE = mgh, KE = 0

2. Momentum & Impulse

Momentum (p) at Impact:

p = m × vf

Impulse (J):

The Impulse required to stop the egg is equal to the change in momentum.

J = Δp = m(vf - vi)

Remember: To save the egg, we want to increase the time of impact to decrease the Force (F = J/t).
