The Car: Rules & Performance

Group Size & Distance Goal

  • Groups of 1-2 students: Min distance 10.0 meters.
  • Groups of 3 students: Min distance 15.0 meters.

Construction Rules

  1. Power: 1 or 2 standard mousetraps (provided). No batteries/elastic bands.
  2. Materials: Completely homemade. NO pre-made kits, Legos, K'nex.
  3. Wheels: At least 3 wheels required.

Report Requirements

Format your lab report professionally with these sections:

  1. Title Page: Names, Date, Title.
  2. Objective: Goal of the project (3-5 sentences).
  3. Procedure & Diagrams: Materials list + Top/Side view drawings.
  4. Data Tables: Raw Data and Calculated Data.
  5. Sample Calculations: Show one example for every formula used.
  6. Physics Theory: 1-2 paragraphs on Energy, Friction, and Newton's Laws.
  7. Results: Outcome summary (5-7 sentences).
  8. Sources: References cited.

Design & Data Collection

Design Considerations

Consider the following variables in your construction:

  • Fin Design: Shape, size, and number of fins affect stability.
  • Water Mass: Too little water provides short thrust; too much weighs it down.
  • Aerodynamics: Nose cone shape reduces air resistance.
